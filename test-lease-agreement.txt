RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is made between John Smith (Landlord) and Jane Doe (Tenant).

PROPERTY: 123 Main Street, Apartment 2B, New York, NY 10001

TERM: This lease begins on January 1, 2024 and ends on December 31, 2024.

RENT: The monthly rent is $2,500.00, due on the 1st of each month.

SECURITY DEPOSIT: Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $5,000.00.

LATE FEE: A late fee of $75.00 will be charged for rent paid after the 5th of the month.

UTILITIES: Tenant is responsible for electricity and gas. Landlord pays for water and trash.

PARKING: One parking space included for an additional $150.00 per month.

NOTICE PERIOD: Either party may terminate this lease with 30 days written notice.

PET POLICY: No pets allowed without written consent. Pet deposit of $500.00 required if approved.

LEASE RENEWAL: Option to renew for one additional year with 60 days notice.
