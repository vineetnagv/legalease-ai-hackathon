# **App Name**: Legalease AI

## Core Features:

- Google Sign-In: Authenticate users securely via Google Sign-In.
- Document Upload: Upload legal documents (PDF, DOCX) with drag-and-drop functionality.
- Role Selection: Specify the user's role in the legal agreement (Tenant, Landlord, etc.).
- AI-Powered Legal Analysis: Leverage AI to analyze uploaded documents and extract key insights. This analysis acts as a tool to derive risk scores and clause explanations.
- Risk Meter: Display a visual gauge indicating the overall risk level (Low, Medium, High) of the document.
- Key Numbers Extraction: Extract and display key numerical data from the document (e.g., rent amount, contract dates).
- Clause Breakdown & Jargon Buster: Show the clauses from the document in plain language and highlight Jargon terms for additional tool-tips.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Trustworthy blue (#4285F4) for main buttons and highlights.
- Background color: Light gray (#F8F9FA) for a clean canvas.
- Text color: Dark charcoal (#202124) for high readability.
- Risk colors: Green (#34A853) for low risk, amber (#FBBC04) for medium risk, and red (#EA4335) for high risk.
- Font family: 'Inter', a sans-serif, will be used for both headlines and body text for high readability and a modern feel.
- Use professional, line-art style icons to represent document types and actions.  Avoid emojis.
- Desktop-first, responsive design that adapts to single-column layouts on mobile devices.
